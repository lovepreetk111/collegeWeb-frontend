    Iskra.Tech 7.0

    Iskra.Tech is an inter-collegiate I.T. event organized by Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (B.Sc-I.T.) students on December 15th, 2018. Iskra.Tech focuses on a variety of technological competitions.
    These events are incredibly innovative and provide a unique alternative to the various college events held in Mumbai.
    This event aims to showcase the student's talent, and we provide them with a platform to do so. To cultivate higher-order thinking abilities such as problem-solving, decision-making, critical thinking, and planning. More than 150 students from Mumbai participate.
    In Iskra.tech 7.0, there were eight different types of competition. Like DIGI-STER (creating digital posters), FRONTEND WIZARD (creating static websites), LOGOTVLING (creating digital logos), OPTICODE.JS, CODE-TECH, TREASURE HUNT, and BLINDTYPING ,TECHNIZIA. "TECHNIZIA" was introduce this year and main focus of our attention this year.
    Technizia is primarily for TYBScIT students; in TY, students must complete a project, either software or hardware related. So we kept the competition going so that they could bring their projects and exhibit them in front of other students and judges. The images clearly show the student's energy and enthusiasm.
    The event was attended by shri shri Dutta Haldankar sir, president of the Chetana Trust, who praised the students and encouraged them.
